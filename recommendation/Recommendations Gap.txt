What?

Its a correlation-based preferences set-up where similar preferences across people are used to understand what they might like.

Based on your similarity, it will identify the gaps in possible interests.

Why?

Helps to profile people with limited starting data and enrich going forward -- feedback loop.

Discovery of the platform and more use across the presentation of events.


How?

Just run the script and a theme will be asked: a list of people that have not selected their preferences will have a likely score based on other preferences.
